Congratulations to Peter, Gromit, Harish, Gustavo, and Brian on their paper: "GALE: Globally Assessing Local Explanations", Peter Xenopoulos, Gromit Chan, Harish Doraiswamy, Luis Gustavo Nonato, Brian Barr, and Claudio Silva. The paper has been selected for a Spotlight Talk at the 2022 ICML Workshop on Topology, Algebra, and Geometry in Machine Learning!
