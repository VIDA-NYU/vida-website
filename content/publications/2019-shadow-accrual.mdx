  We present Shadow Accrual Maps, a technique for efficiently computing and
  visualizing the accumulation of shadows over time in urban environments. Our
  approach enables city-scale analysis of shadow patterns, supporting
  applications in urban planning, real estate analysis, and environmental
  studies.
