ITK-SNAP is a software application used to segment structures in 3D medical images. It is the product of years of work to create a tool that would be dedicated to a specific function, segmentation, and would be easy to use and learn.

## Features

- **3D Segmentation**: Segment anatomical structures in medical images
- **Multi-Platform**: Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux
- **Free and Open-Source**: Available to everyone
- **Easy to Learn**: Intuitive interface for medical professionals

## Applications

ITK-SNAP is widely used in medical imaging research for:
- Brain structure segmentation
- Tumor delineation
- Organ segmentation
- Research and clinical applications
