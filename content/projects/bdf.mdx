The Biomedical Data Fabric (BDF) Toolbox program aims to simplify the integration of biomedical research data from different sources, addressing challenges posed by incompatible data formats and standards.

## Goals

- Simplify integration of biomedical research data
- Address incompatible data formats and standards
- Enable researchers to work with diverse data sources seamlessly
