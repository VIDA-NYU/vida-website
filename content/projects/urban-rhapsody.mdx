Urban Rhapsody is a system for large-scale exploration of urban soundscapes. It enables researchers and urban planners to understand the acoustic environment of cities.

## Features

- **Soundscape Visualization**: Visual exploration of urban sound data
- **Large-Scale Analysis**: Handle city-wide acoustic data
- **Interactive Exploration**: Navigate through soundscapes interactively
- **Pattern Discovery**: Identify acoustic patterns in urban environments
