GoViral is a project focused on generating community-sourced disease data. The project enables participatory disease surveillance through citizen reporting.

## Goals

- Track respiratory infections through community participation
- Generate real-time disease surveillance data
- Enable research on disease spread patterns
- Engage citizens in public health monitoring
