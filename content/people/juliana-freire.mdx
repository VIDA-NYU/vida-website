Juliana Freire is a Professor of Computer Science and Engineering and Data Science at New York University. She is an Institute Professor and one of the co-directors of the VIDA Center. Her research interests are in large-scale data analysis, web data management, and computational reproducibility. She was elected an AAAS Fellow in 2022.
