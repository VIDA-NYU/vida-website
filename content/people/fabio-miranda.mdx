Fabio Miranda was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the VIDA Center. His research focused on urban visualization and visual analytics. He is now an Assistant Professor at the University of Illinois Chicago. He is a co-author of Urban Pulse, Shadow Accrual Maps, Urban Mosaic, and Urban Rhapsody.
