Patrick Yubeaton is a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at NYU Tandon. He is advised by Dr. Quanyan Zhu and is a member of the Laboratory for Agile and Resilient Complex Systems (LARX) group. He completed his BS in Electrical Engineering at Rutgers University in New Brunswick in 2022. He is interested in cyber physical systems, human computer interaction, and cognitive security.
