Claudio Silva is an Institute Professor of Computer Science and Engineering and Data Science at New York University. He is one of the co-directors of the VIDA Center. His research focuses on visualization, graphics, and urban computing. He was named an ACM Fellow in 2023 for contributions to visualization, geometry processing, and urban computing.
