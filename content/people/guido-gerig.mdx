Guido Gerig is an Institute Professor at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. His research focuses on development of novel imaging analysis methodologies related to segmentation, shape analysis, and image statistics. His work is multidisciplinary, primarily collaborating with experts in clinical imaging on research in areas including schizophrenia, autism, and multiple sclerosis.
