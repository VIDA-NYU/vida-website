Apoorv Vikram Singh is a Ph.D. candidate at the department of Computer Science at the NYU Tandon School of Engineering. He is part of the Algorithms and Foundations Group, where he is being advised by Dr Chris Musco.
Before joining NYU, he was a visiting researcher at the MODAL team in INRIA Lille where he worked with Dr Hemant Tyagi and Dr Mihai Cucuringu (Univ of Oxford). Before that he was a Project Associate in the Dept. of Computer Science and Automation, Indian Institute of Science, where he worked with Dr Anand Louis and Dr Amit Deshpande (Microsoft Research, India). He obtained his undergraduate degree from IIIT Bangalore.
