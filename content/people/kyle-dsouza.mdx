Kyle Dsouza is an M.S. graduate in Computer Science from the NYU Tandon School of Engineering, where he worked at the Visualization and Data Analytics Research Center (VIDA) under the supervision of Prof. Robert Krueger. His research focused on data visualization and visual analytics, with an emphasis on biomedical image analysis and spatial transcriptomics, including the development of interactive visualization pipelines for high-dimensional HIV patient imaging data. He is currently working as a Software Engineer in the FinTech space in New York City, building data-driven systems at the intersection of finance and technology.
