Rumi Chunara is an Associate Professor at NYU with appointments in Computer Science and Engineering and Biostatistics. Her research focuses on developing computational and statistical approaches for acquiring, integrating and using data to improve population-level public health. She received an NSF CAREER Award in 2019.
